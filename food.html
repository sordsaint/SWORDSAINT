Chicken and Rice
I LOVE chicken and rice. Now I have the means to make really good chicken and rice, but when I was a kid, I grew up really poor. When my grandma first got custody of me, she would make me frozen chicken nuggets cut up in rice. For years that was my favorite meal, and even now, at 18, I still will make frozen chicken nuggets cut up in rice just as a way to honor my grandma. R.I.P Jean, I love you grandma.
